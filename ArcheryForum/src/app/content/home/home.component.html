The Quest for the Elusive Rubber-Tipped Arrow

In the mystical land of Quiverwood, Sir Blunderfeather challenged Lady Bullseye to a duel.
The goal? To hit the moon first. Sir Blunderfeather's unconventional rubber-tipped arrow ricocheted off clouds,
penguins, and the Royal Jester's nose. Lady Bullseye's traditional arrow grazed the moon's chin, leaving a faint blush.
The crowd erupted in laughter, and the moon, unimpressed, continued its lunar business.
